Trust through Transparency

The OpenPDI project is an open-source effort to standardize and present data on police activity in the U.S.

Our goal is to make it easier for journalists, researchers, and citizens to explore police-related data at the department, state, and national level.

100% Reproducible

Every data source we standardize, use, or present is freely available to the public.

Open Source

Our source code, publications, and website are all open source and available on GitHub.
